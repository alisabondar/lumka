# Lumka — Evolve Your Fox

A strategic card game where you evolve your fox through 6 rounds of challenges, building traits and managing stability to become immortal.

## How to Play

1. **Start** with a hand of 6 cards from a 52-card deck.
2. **Build your fox**: double-click cards to apply traits; click to select for discard. Drawing and discarding cost stability.
3. **Face challenges**: each round offers 3 random challenges—pick one and meet its requirements (traits, score, stability) to advance.
4. **Survive**: stability starts at 10 and drops with each action. Complete all 6 rounds to win.

**Traits**: 🩶 Flourish (+2 score, +1 stability) · ⚙️ Adapt (+1 score) · ⛓️ Burden (+1 score, -2 stability) · ✨ Catalyst (special effects)

## Tech

- **Stack**: Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS 4, MUI, Supabase
- **Game**: 100 challenges across 10 tiers, immutable state, full test coverage
- **UX**: Dark mode, Lottie animations, walkthrough, persistent scoreboard, accessibility
